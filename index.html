My name is Leonardo Castro, originally from the city of Reynosa, Tamaulipas, Mexico. I am passionate about programming, always learning new things in various disciplines, strongly guided by my intuition and imagination.

I had my first encounter with development and programming at my 13 years old with the LUA programming language, working on an open-source game engine called The Forgotten Server. Since then, I have been acquiring more and better skills at different points throughout my life.

I have also learned a bit in other disciplines related to programming, such as graphic design, music theory, and creative writing.

From a young age, I have been dissatisfied with the inadequate education system in my country. As a result, most of my knowledge, despite attending school, has been self-taught.

Currently, I am developing code in various programming languages such as PHP, Go, and JavaScript, with the goal of growing as a programmer and securing a good job or joining a development company.

Mainly Goals

Enhance my skills in the Go programming language

Since I discovered the Go programming language, I have been fascinated by it. I am determined to develop more applications using this language and its development environment. Becoming a senior backend and DevOps engineer is one of my most fervent goals.

Develop and maintain my own open source project

As the main creator of the MixiPHP project (now DEPRECATED), I would like to see one of my personal project growing and creating a community of open-source developers while maintaining its integrity and ensuring a lightweight and contemporary tool.

Secure a position working on a project abroad

I want to participate and gain experience working in different companies that develop software in various categories. I enjoy programming challenges and, above all, learning and developing myself as a programmer.

Create and officially publish a game on a current popular platform.

Being passionate about video games, one of my current goals is to publish a game developed by myself on a platform like Steam or itch.io. My intention is to continue improving my skills as a software developer by understanding the architecture and logic behind a video game.
